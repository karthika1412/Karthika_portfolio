Circheal Studio Portfolio website

Position :

Web Application Developer.

Timeline :

April 2023 - June 2023

Team Size :

3 Designers and 2 Developers

Tools & Technology

Figma for Design
HTML,CSS,JS

Role

I was responsible for wireframing and designing the entire website. My goal was to effectively showcase the studio's completed projects and services while ensuring a seamless user experience. I worked under the guidance of my senior throughout the project.

Challenges

  • Showcase every project effectively to attract users and encourage them to explore more of Circheal Studio's works.
